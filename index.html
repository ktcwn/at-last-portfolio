THINGS I LOVE DOING

Design

Learning, researching and implementing UI/UX trends to the design of a website is just as important to me as the functionality of a website. I love working with clients on projects geared toward the product and customer through visual and user-friendly experiences.

Development

As a developer with an educational background in the MERN stack, I am able to create full-stack web applications, with a particular passion in front-end development and design. I love curating a web space for customers and clients to enjoy visually and effectively.

Marketing

I firmly believe that the understanding of marketing is crucial when building a site for a client. Despite formal marketing experience, I enjoy researching marketing trends and how to implement them into my development and designs to boost web traffic.
